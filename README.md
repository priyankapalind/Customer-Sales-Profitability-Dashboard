# 📊 Customer Sales & Profitability Dashboard | Tableau

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard that analyzes customer sales performance, profitability, and purchasing behavior using the Sample Superstore dataset.

The dashboard enables business users to identify top-performing customers, monitor monthly sales trends, compare customer segments, and evaluate customer profitability through interactive visualizations.

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard answers the following business questions:

- How are sales performing throughout the year?
- Who are the top revenue-generating customers?
- Which customers generate high sales but low profit?
- Which customer segment contributes the highest sales?
- Which segment has the highest profit margin?
- What are the overall business KPIs?

---

# 📈 Dashboard KPIs

- 👥 Total Customers
- 📦 Total Orders
- 💰 Total Revenue
- 📊 Net Profit
- 📉 Profit Margin
- 🛒 Average Order Value

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales.
- Home Office customers achieve the highest profit margin.
- Sales reach their peak during the final quarter of the year.
- High sales do not always result in high profitability.
- A few customers generate significant revenue while contributing little or negative profit.
